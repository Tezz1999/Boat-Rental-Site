Placid Boat Rentals is in Lake Placid, New York in the heart of the Adirondacks.   

Placid Boat Rentals is strategically located between Lake Placid Lake and Mirror Lake.  Offering convenient and easy access to the lake of your choice.  Take advantage of what Lake Placid Lake has to offer by  motor or paddle boat.  Or enjoy the serenity of Mirror Lake from a paddle boat of choice.
